SQL Top 3 Students Query

Description

In this query, we store the marks of students and calculate the top 3 students.
Criteria: Student should have an aggregate of more than 60%, must not fail any subject (i.e. no marks < 30), and must not be absent in any subject.

Example

  • Exactly 4 students failing overall
  • 2 students marked absent in one subject each
  • One of the absentees having the highest aggregate
  • Aggregate and percentage columns included
